What is Pupillary Distance for Glasses and Why Does It Matter?

During your eye exam, ask for your Pupillary Distance (PD). You'll see this term on every online eyewear site. Your PD is simply the distance, in millimeters, between the centers of your pupils.

Why is this tiny number so important? It ensures the optical center of your lenses aligns perfectly with your pupils. An incorrect PD can lead to eye strain, headaches, and distorted vision—essentially making your new prescription sunglasses useless. During our research, we found that while some websites offer online tools to measure your PD, the most precise and reliable method is to get this measurement directly from your optometrist. It removes all guesswork.
