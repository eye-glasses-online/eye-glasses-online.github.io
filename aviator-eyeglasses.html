Presbyopia is a natural age-related condition affecting your eyes' ability to focus on nearby objects. It's a gradual process, typically beginning around age 40, impacting nearly everyone eventually. What are bifocal glasses for? This common vision change is caused by a loss of elasticity in the eye's lens. As we age, the lens within our eye hardens and loses its ability to change shape effectively.
